Welcome to Project Manager, the project management website that allows you to effortlessly oversee your projects. With Project Manager, you can create project workspaces where you can manage your projects, including three free workspaces. You can also add members (maximum of 5 people, including yourself) to the workspace. Within the workspace, you can create tasks, assign them to members, track their progress, and modify their descriptions. Additionally, you can capture photos of the task board to share with colleagues who are included in the project. Simplify your project management with Project Manager today!
